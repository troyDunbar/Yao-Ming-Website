Yao Ming

Yao Ming was a professional basketball court who played for the Houston Rockets from 2002 - 2011.


Ming was the first overall pick in the 2002 NBA draft, coming from the Shanghai Sharks in his home country of China. Known for his height, standing at 7 ft 6 in, he instatnlty stood out as a dangerous forward and rebounder.


Yao Ming retired in 2011 after several seasons of being riddled with ankle and foot injuries. Being inducted into the NBA Hall of Fame in 2016, Yao Ming has become the image of Chinese basketball coming to America.
