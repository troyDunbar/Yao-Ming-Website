Childhood

Yao Ming was born September 12, 1980 in Shanghai China. Born weighing 11.0 lbs he was more then twice the size of the average Chinese newborn.


Yao is the only child of, 6 ft 7 in, Yao Zhiyuan and 6 ft 3 in, Fang Fengdi, both of whom were former professional basketball players.


He began playing professional basketball at 16 years old for the Shaghai Sharks. Averaging 39 points a game after his first three seasons and being the most dominant player in the league.
